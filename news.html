MASTER CHAMPIONS

Team Liquid only dropped one map all tournament coming against G2 Esports in the group stage. The best CS:GO team in the world only lost double digit rounds three times all tournament in maps it won. Team Liquid secured the first championship in Season 3 of the Intel Grand Slam. New rules were implemented after Team Liquid won Season 2 adding a new condition to the bonus $1 million. Players must either win six non-Masters level tournaments or four including one Masters level event.
